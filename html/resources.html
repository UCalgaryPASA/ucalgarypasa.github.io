Resources

Club Resources


Club Room
PASA offers a space where members are welcome to come by to academically and socially connect with each other. Depending on executive availability, the club room is often open weekdays between roughly between 8am and 8pm (when people are around). Come find us in ST 058-060!

Mentorship
We offer a mentorship program for new students where individuals are paired with an experienced member of similar interests for personal and academic assistance. Please visit our Discord server or speak with an executive member for more information and the sign-up sheet.

Tutorial Night
We offer a weekly tutorial night for students to come by and get help with their courses. Hosted by executives E and Abby, this is a great opportunity ask questions about courses, labs, concepts, schedules, and more! It is available on discord and in the PASA club room. More details can be found in the announcements channel on our discord server.

Support Services
The University of Calgary also offers a variety of support services for students.

  • Mental Health Services
  • Wellness Supports
  • Student Accessibility Services
  • Undergraduate Science Center

Reach Out


The anonymous survey below is for you to express concerns within the Physics and Astronomy department anonymously through PASA. Please remember that you are always welcome to reach out to the resources we suggest on your own if you are comfortable, but if you would like to remain anonymous and have PASA representatives speak on your behalf, you may use this form. You do not need to be a member of PASA to use this form!
