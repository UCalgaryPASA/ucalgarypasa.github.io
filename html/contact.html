Membership


If you are interested in joining PASA, come visit the club room in Science Theatres 058/060 during a school day and talk to one of our exec members for a membership (see the About Us page for the execs). If no one is around, email us at physastr@ucalgary.ca and we will setup a time where an exec will be around to meet you.

PASA Room


The PASA room is located in Science Theatres 058 and 060. When walking into ST from Science A, descend the flight of stairs into the basement. Walk straight and turn left at the second hallway. Proceed down that hallway for approximately 30 meters and the room will be on your left.

See the UCalgary Interactive Room Finder for a map of campus.
