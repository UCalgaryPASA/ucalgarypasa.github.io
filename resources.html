PASA offers programs and support so students can succeed. Members can come to the club room (ST 058-060) weekdays betweeen ~8am and ~8pm for homework and physics course assistance.

In addition, we offer a mentorship program for new students. Individuals are paired with an experienced member of similar interests for personal and academic assistance. Please visit our Discord server or speak with an executive member for more information and the sign-up sheet.
